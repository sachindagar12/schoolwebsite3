Our school

Pratibha schools follow the national curriculum, adapted and adjusted as necessary to meet the needs of the children. A good many have in-house care from a range of therapists, which may include a speech and language therapist (SaLT), an occupational therapist and a physiotherapist,or will have a NHS therapist assigned to the school.

Pratibha Secondary School, established in the year 2003, is a premier school under the aegis of the National Society, New Delhi. Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), the institution has an educational policy that is dynamic in approach and in accordance with the changing needs of the society. The school aims at providing a conducive environment to stimulate intellectual growth of each child.
